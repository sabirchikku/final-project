About Us

KSM Shajahan Saquafi Kakkanad

Jamia Ash`ariyya is a spiritual cum material educational centre, founded in 1994. It has existed as an asylum of orphans. But we could not continue this institution for the orphans alone. Because we came to know there were a large number of needy, helpless, poor people who found it extremely difficult to pull on without help from others. We felt it was our duty to provide them with help and assistance and as a result Jamia Ash`ariya became a leading institution for the orphans and poors. Because of the blessing of the god almighty, we were able to make remarkable progress. We are running many institution like Sharee`ath College, Da-a-wa College, Orphan and destitute home, Hifzul Qur`an college, High School, Dars and Computer centre etc
